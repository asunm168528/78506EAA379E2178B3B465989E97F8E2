Egg Fried Rice Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of cooked rice (preferably cold)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of oil (vegetable or sesame oil)
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup of peas (frozen or fresh)
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon of sesame oil (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Green onions for garnish (optional)

Instructions

Step 1:

Heat a pan or wok over medium heat. Add 1 tablespoon of oil and scramble the eggs. Once scrambled, remove them from the pan and set aside.

Step 2:

In the same pan, add a little more oil if necessary and saut√© the chopped onion and minced garlic until soft and fragrant (about 2 minutes).

Step 3:

Add the peas and cook for another 2 minutes until they are heated through.

Step 4:

Add the cold rice to the pan and stir well. Break up any clumps of rice to ensure it heats evenly.

Step 5:

Return the scrambled eggs to the pan, and add the soy sauce and sesame oil (if using). Stir everything together and cook for another 2 minutes.

Step 6:

Season with salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with chopped green onions if desired. Serve hot!
