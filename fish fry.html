Fish Fry Recipe

About the Recipe

This delicious fish fry is perfect for a weekend meal or any occasion. It is crispy on the outside, tender on the inside, and packed with flavors. Follow the recipe below to make your own fish fry at home!

Ingredients

  • 4 pieces of fish fillets (like tilapia or cod)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 1 egg
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)
  • Lemon wedges (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a shallow bowl, mix the flour, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  2. In another bowl, whisk together the buttermilk and egg.
  3. Dip the fish fillets into the buttermilk mixture, then dredge them in the flour mixture, ensuring they are fully coated.
  4. Heat about 1 inch of vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  5. Once the oil is hot, carefully place the coated fish fillets in the skillet and fry for 3-4 minutes per side or until golden brown and crispy.
  6. Remove the fish from the skillet and place on a plate lined with paper towels to drain excess oil.
  7. Serve immediately with lemon wedges and enjoy!
