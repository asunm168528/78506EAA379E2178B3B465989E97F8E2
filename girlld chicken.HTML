Delicious Grilled Chicken Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 Chicken breasts
  • 1 tbsp Olive oil
  • 1 tsp Paprika
  • 1 tsp Garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Black pepper
  • 1 tbsp Lemon juice
  • Fresh parsley (for garnish)

Preparation Steps:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, mix the olive oil, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Rub the chicken breasts with the spice mixture on both sides.
  4. Place the chicken on the grill and cook for about 5-7 minutes on each side, until the chicken reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C).
  5. Drizzle with lemon juice and garnish with fresh parsley.
  6. Serve hot with your favorite side dish.
