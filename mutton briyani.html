Mutton Biryani Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g Mutton (cut into pieces)
  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 1 large onion (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 tablespoons ginger garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 cup fresh coriander leaves
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 4-5 cloves
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 4-5 green cardamoms
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon red chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon biryani masala powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 tablespoons ghee or vegetable oil
  • Water (for cooking rice)

Instructions:

  • 1. Wash and soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes.
  • 2. In a large pot, heat ghee or oil. Add bay leaves, cloves, cinnamon, and cardamoms. Sauté for a few seconds.
  • 3. Add sliced onions and cook until golden brown.
  • 4. Add ginger garlic paste and sauté for 2 minutes.
  • 5. Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and biryani masala. Cook until tomatoes are soft and oil separates.
  • 6. Add the mutton pieces and cook until browned on all sides.
  • 7. Add yogurt, fresh coriander, and mint leaves. Mix well and cook for another 5 minutes.
  • 8. In a separate pot, bring water to a boil. Add salt and the soaked rice. Cook until rice is 70% cooked, then drain the water.
  • 9. Layer the cooked rice on top of the mutton mixture. Cover with a tight-fitting lid and cook on low heat (Dum) for about 20-25 minutes.
  • 10. Remove from heat and let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.
