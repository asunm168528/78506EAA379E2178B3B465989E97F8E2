Nandu Roast Recipe (Crab Roast)

Ingredients

  • 1 medium-sized crab (Nandu)
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, finely chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Curry leaves
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Clean and crack the crab into pieces, removing the shell and gills. Keep the crab meat aside.
  2. Heat oil in a large pan. Add curry leaves and sauté for a minute.
  3. Add the finely chopped onion and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Stir in the ginger-garlic paste and sauté for another minute until fragrant.
  5. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until soft and mushy.
  6. Mix in the turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and garam masala. Stir well to combine.
  7. Add the crab meat pieces to the pan and mix it well with the spices. Cook for 5-10 minutes, allowing the crab to absorb the flavors.
  8. Season with salt to taste and cook until the crab is fully roasted and cooked through.
  9. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or roti.
