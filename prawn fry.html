Prawn Fry Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g fresh prawns, peeled and deveined
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil (vegetable or coconut oil)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish)

Cooking Steps:

  1. In a bowl, mix prawns with ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, lemon juice, and salt. Let it marinate for about 15-20 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
  3. Add the green chilies and sauté for another minute.
  4. Add the marinated prawns to the pan and cook on medium-high heat for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the prawns turn pink and are fully cooked.
  5. Once done, garnish with freshly chopped coriander leaves.
  6. Serve hot with steamed rice or flatbread of your choice.
